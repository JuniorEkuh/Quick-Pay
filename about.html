About QuickPay

What is QuickPay?

Quickpay is a free to use site, where you can send money with ease without the stress of dialing any code.

Why use QuickPay?

QuickPay helps you to do all necessary dialing and selections, where by the user need to just input the credentials of the person he/she wants to send money to. So you won't have to go to your dialer and start dialing codes and following instructions to send someone money, QuickPay does the dialing for you.

QuickPay Is Free

QuickPay is, and will always be, a completely free to use site.
